SKATE CLUB

brooklyn boulders queensbridge

what is skate club?

Skate club is a club through Brooklyn Boulders Queensbridge run by Yuma Sado.
We meet up at a rink weekly for people to skate together.
Everyone is welcome!! It does not matter if you have skated before or not - skate club is a place for anyone to come skate, learn new tricks, and be a part of an amazing community.

Follow our instagram for consistent updates!

how to join: open to all bkb members!

need to bring: skates/blades | protective gear

when? every saturday at noon

where? dutch kills rink

upcoming events: October 14, October 21, October 28
